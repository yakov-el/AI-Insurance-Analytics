Messaging Strategies by Lead Segment (Messaging by Segment) [Doc C1]Objective: Tailoring marketing language and specific pain points to the segment.Young Adults (Under 35): Focus on Affordability, flexibility, and future growth enablement. Message: "Insurance that grows with your life."Families (35-55): Focus on Family Security, financial stability in case of disaster, and comprehensive coverage. Message: "Peace of mind for those you cherish most."Seniors (55+): Focus on Asset Protection, Estate Planning, and long-term care solutions. Message: "Preserving the legacy you've built."Language Adaptation: Avoid overly technical terms in the initial outreach to maximize engagement.
