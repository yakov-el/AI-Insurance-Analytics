Grace Period and Late Payment Policy [Doc L1]Objective: Minimize policy lapse due to temporary payment failures.Policy Guidelines:Immediate Notification: An SMS and email notification must be sent immediately following the original payment failure.Standard Grace Period: The customer is entitled to a 30-day Grace Period from the original due date. The insurance coverage remains fully active during this period.Proactive Outreach (Day 15): The retention agent must initiate a personal phone call after 15 days into the grace period to understand the financial status and offer immediate solutions.Policy Reinstatement: A policy canceled due to non-payment can be reinstated up to 90 days from the cancellation date, contingent upon payment of all accrued debts. Using the term "Grace Period" in communication improves clarity.
