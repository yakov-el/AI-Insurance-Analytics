Touchpoint Cadence and Automation [Doc C2]Objective: Establishing a consistent, non-intrusive presence with leads.7-Day Lead Nurturing Plan (Cadence):Day 0 (Registration): Immediate confirmation + core Value Proposition.Day 2: Email featuring Testimonials and Social Proof from similar customers.Day 4: Short, non-invasive phone call from a representative (only if the lead showed high intent). If no answer, send an SMS.Day 7: Final email before closing the Cadence, emphasizing a limited-time incentive or "last chance opportunity".Golden Rule: High Intent leads must be handled within one hour; cold leads enter a 3-month automation program. Adhering to this Cadence is crucial.
