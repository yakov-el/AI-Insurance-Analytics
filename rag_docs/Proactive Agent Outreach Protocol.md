Proactive Agent Outreach Protocol (Agent Outreach) [Doc L2]Objective: Building personal rapport and early identification of financial distress or dissatisfaction.Outreach Procedure:Identification (High Risk): Focus exclusively on customers flagged with a lapse probability of 85% or higher or those who have failed two payment attempts.Tone and Approach: The conversation must be empathetic, non-judgemental, and focused on "shared solution finding". Avoid hard-selling.Escalation: If the customer clearly expresses intent to cancel, immediately escalate the case to a Retention Manager for a "final counter-offer".Documentation: Every interaction, including phone calls, emails, or SMS, must be documented in the CRM system as an "Agent Outreach Log".
