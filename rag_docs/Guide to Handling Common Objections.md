Guide to Handling Common Objections [Doc C3]

Objective: Providing agents with tools to neutralize psychological and financial barriers.

"It's Too Expensive" (Price Objection):

Response: Perform an apples-to-apples comparison. "Our price reflects superior coverage in [Specific Area]. Let's see how we can tailor the policy to your budget by adjusting the deductible."

"I Don't Need It Now" (Timing Objection):

Response: "The only thing preventing you from getting coverage in the future is an unexpected event that changes your medical status. It is better to secure this while you are young and healthy."

"I Need to Speak to My Spouse/Partner":

Response: "I completely understand. Perhaps we can schedule a joint call so I can address all their questions? Family security is the most important decision."
