Loyalty Discounts for Tenured Customers (Loyalty Discounts) [Doc L4]Objective: Reward high-tenure customers and emphasize their long-term value to the company.Discount Guidelines:3+ Years Tenure: Automatic 5% discount on upcoming administration fees.5+ Years Tenure: 10% discount on the next policy renewal or the option for a free additional cover for one year (e.g., basic travel insurance).Gold Customers (10+ Years): Receive a dedicated Account Manager and a specific Loyalty Discount at renewal.Value Emphasis: Agents must clearly articulate the exclusive benefits available only to these loyal customers compared to new customers.
