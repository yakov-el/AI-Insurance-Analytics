Flexible Payment Plan Options (Payment Plans) [Doc L3]Objective: Prevent lapse when customers are facing limited cash flow.Types of Plans:Debt Split: Dividing a large monthly payment into smaller, manageable installments over a period (up to 6 months).Payment Deferral: Deferring one payment for up to 90 days, subject to managerial approval (typically for tenured customers of 3+ years). Avoid deferral if it creates an credit risk.Seasonal Payment Arrangement: Adjusting payment dates to align with seasonal income (e.g., concentrated payment after harvest or peak tourism season).Frequency Change: Switching from monthly to quarterly or annual payment frequency (with a small discount) to reduce administration costs and prevent difficult monthly payments.
