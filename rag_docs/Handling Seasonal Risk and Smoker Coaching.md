Handling Seasonal Risk and Smoker Coaching [Doc L5]Objective: Providing personalized solutions for specific lapse risks related to lifestyle or time of year.Seasonal Risk: Identify customers with seasonal income (agriculture, tourism). Agent Outreach (Doc L2) should be initiated one month before their expected financial "drought" period, offering Payment Plans (Doc L3).Smoker Coaching: Customers classified as smokers pay higher premiums. Offer them a Smoker Cessation Coaching Program (in partnership with an external institute).Quitting Benefit: If the customer provides medical confirmation of being smoke-free for 12 months, they qualify for a policy review and a dramatic Premium Reduction ("risk category change"). Communicate the potential for premium reduction as a significant incentive.
